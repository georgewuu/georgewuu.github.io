What truly separates a lucky deal from elite poker strategy? This post explores a compelling mathematical framework designed to isolate "luck equity" during the deal from "skill equity" during the betting rounds. But as top analysts point out, the math isn't nearly as clean as it looks. Discover the hidden statistical vulnerabilities of the game , the realities of high variance , and why macro-strategic "game selection" remains a player's ultimate edge.
