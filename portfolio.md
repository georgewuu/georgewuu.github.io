### Automating Veterinary Radiology Report Classification Using LLMs
* **Type:** Presentation deck
* **Abstract:** This presentation outlines a practical workflow for evaluating large language models on veterinary radiology report classification. The project focuses on identifying specific thorax and abdomen diseases from unstructured reports, comparing model behavior across multiple LLMs, and assessing performance with sensitivity and specificity as the primary evaluation metrics.
